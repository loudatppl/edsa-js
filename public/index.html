Welcome to EDSA! In this game, you'll be driving a car and your goal is to avoid crashing into other cars for as long as possible. The longer you can avoid crashing, the higher your score will be. So, buckle up and get ready to hit the road!

Instructions

  1. 1. To start the game, press play.
  2. 2. Use the arrow keys(⬅⬆➡⬇) to move.
  3. 3. Avoid crashing into cars. If you crash, the game is over
